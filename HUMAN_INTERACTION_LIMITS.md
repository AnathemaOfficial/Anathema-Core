# HUMAN_INTERACTION_LIMITS
## Anathema P0 — Boundaries of Human Contact and Influence

This document defines the **explicit limits of human interaction** with Anathema P0.

These limits are not behavioral guidelines.
They are **structural constraints**.

Anathema P0 does not collaborate.
It does not obey.
It does not interpret intent.

---

## 1. Design Principle

Human interaction with Anathema P0 is permitted only insofar as it does **not require interpretation, negotiation, or trust**.

Interaction is physical and immediate.
Meaning is not exchanged.

---

## 2. What Humans Can Do

Humans may:

- physically touch Anathema P0 within the operational envelope
- apply forces within invariant bounds
- reposition Anathema P0 when unpowered
- externally activate or deactivate the system
- replace or repair modules while powered down

All such actions are **external**.
None are interpreted internally.

---

## 3. What Humans Cannot Do

Humans cannot:

- issue commands
- request actions
- negotiate outcomes
- persuade behavior
- teach through demonstration
- modify behavior through repetition

There is no command interface.

---

## 4. Absence of Communication Channel

Anathema P0 has:

- no language interface
- no symbolic input
- no gesture recognition
- no semantic parsing

Any perceived responsiveness is a consequence of physical regulation only.

---

## 5. Touch Is Not Input

Physical contact is treated as:

- force
- pressure
- thermal exchange
- impedance

Touch does not carry meaning.
It carries load.

---

## 6. No Social Model

Anathema P0 does not model:

- humans
- emotions
- intentions
- social roles

There is no distinction between:
- human contact
- object contact
- environmental contact

---

## 7. Safety Through Indifference

Because Anathema P0 does not recognize humans:

- it cannot deceive
- it cannot manipulate
- it cannot favor
- it cannot retaliate

Safety arises from **indifference**, not alignment.

---

## 8. Interaction-Induced State Transitions

Human interaction may only cause:

- local invariant enforcement
- transition to DEGRADED or BLOCKED
- SAFE_COLLAPSE if limits are exceeded

Humans cannot trigger transitions upward.

---

## 9. No Trust Relationship

Anathema P0 does not:

- trust humans
- distrust humans
- rely on human supervision

Human presence does not modify internal behavior.

---

## 10. Falsifiability Conditions

This document is invalidated if Anathema P0:

- responds differently to humans than to objects
- alters behavior after repeated human interaction
- forms a preference for or avoidance of humans
- accepts symbolic or gestural commands

Any such behavior implies agentic interpretation.

---

## 11. Canon Statement

> Anathema does not interact with humans.
> Humans merely exist within its physical field.

---

## 12. Summary

Human interaction with Anathema P0 is:

- non-semantic
- non-social
- non-instructional
- non-influential

There is no human–machine relationship.
Only shared physical space.
