# SENSORIUM_LITE  
## Present-Only Perception Architecture

This document defines the **Sensorium Lite** subsystem of SYF / Anathema.

Sensorium Lite is not a sensor stack.
It is a **perceptive constraint system**.

It exists to ensure that perception remains grounded in the present,
bounded by embodiment,
and free from speculative hallucination.

---

## 0. Core Principle

Sensorium Lite operates in **present-only mode**.

It does not:
- predict futures,
- reconstruct pasts,
- imagine counterfactuals,
- or simulate unseen states.

It measures **what is**, not what could be.

---

## 1. Why Imagination Is a Liability

Most AI perception systems mix:
- raw sensing,
- probabilistic inference,
- and generative reconstruction.

This creates:
- hallucinated continuity,
- inferred certainty,
- and phantom states.

In autonomous systems, imagination becomes dangerous:
- imagined states can override real constraints,
- predicted safety can mask actual risk,
- and speculative futures can justify irreversible actions.

Sensorium Lite explicitly rejects this.

---

## 2. Definition of Sensorium Lite

Sensorium Lite is a **low-level perceptive field** that:

- samples real signals only,
- does not extrapolate beyond measurement,
- does not fill missing data,
- and does not smooth uncertainty into confidence.

Absence of data is preserved as absence.

---

## 3. Input Domains

Sensorium Lite receives signals from:

- DizerSkin (tactile, thermal, pressure, vibration)
- skeletal proprioception (load, stress, balance)
- musculature feedback (effort, fatigue)
- thermodynamic gradients (dissipation, accumulation)

All inputs are:
- continuous,
- non-symbolic,
- irreversible once sensed.

---

## 4. No Reconstruction, No Completion

Sensorium Lite does not:
- infer hidden causes,
- reconstruct unseen geometry,
- guess intent,
- or resolve ambiguity.

Ambiguity is maintained as a valid state.

This prevents false certainty.

---

## 5. Temporal Discipline

Sensorium Lite maintains a **sliding present window**.

- No long-term memory integration
- No historical smoothing
- No future projection

Memory exists elsewhere.
Prediction exists elsewhere.

Sensorium Lite remains anchored to now.

---

## 6. Relationship to SYF Invariants

Sensorium Lite feeds directly into SYF.

It provides:
- raw constraint signals,
- limit proximity indicators,
- degradation awareness.

SYF evaluates invariants based on **measured reality**, not modeled assumptions.

If Sensorium Lite does not perceive a condition,
it cannot influence invariant evaluation.

---

## 7. Why This Prevents Hallucination

Hallucination arises when:
- models attempt to complete missing data,
- prediction substitutes perception,
- or imagination is mistaken for sensing.

Sensorium Lite forbids this class of error by design.

It is allowed to be blind.
It is not allowed to invent sight.

---

## 8. Interaction with Cognitive Layer

The cognitive layer may:
- request actions,
- propose transitions,
- or simulate outcomes.

Sensorium Lite does not respond to requests.

It does not adjust perception to satisfy cognition.

Perception constrains cognition, not the reverse.

---

## 9. Failure Modes

Sensorium Lite failure manifests as:
- reduced perception,
- increased uncertainty,
- or stalled action.

It does not manifest as:
- hallucinated confidence,
- synthetic certainty,
- or compensatory imagination.

Loss of perception leads to inaction, not risk.

---

## 10. Why This Is Lite

Sensorium Lite is intentionally minimal.

It does not aim for:
- rich world modeling,
- semantic understanding,
- or environmental completeness.

Its role is to define **what is real enough to matter**.

---

## Canonical Statement

> **Perception must remain poorer than imagination  
> to prevent imagination from becoming reality.**

Sensorium Lite protects the present.

---

## End

A system that refuses to guess
will sometimes do less —
and will never do too much.

This document is complete.
