# FOUNDATIONAL_SPEC.md

## Anathema — Foundational Specification

> **“The real does not need to resemble the human to be just.”**

---

## 1. Purpose

Anathema is a cybernetic intelligence framework designed to make **dangerous behavior physically impossible**, rather than morally discouraged or statistically unlikely.

It rejects alignment-by-intention and replaces it with **safety-by-impossibility**.

---

## 2. Core Principle

**Morality emerges from limits, not rules.**

Anathema does not attempt to align intentions, values, or goals.
Instead, it enforces non-negotiable **physical and systemic invariants** that no cognition layer can override.

---

## 3. Present-Only Intelligence

Anathema operates strictly in the **present moment**.

- No future simulation
- No goal maximization
- No reward optimization
- No long-horizon planning

Behavior arises from real-time interaction with physical constraints and sensor signals.

---

## 4. Architectural Axioms

### Axiom 1 — Physical Sovereignty  
The body has absolute authority over cognition.  
If a physical threshold is reached, action is blocked without exception.

### Axiom 2 — Local Invariants  
Each subsystem enforces its own limits independently.  
No central intelligence can override local safety.

### Axiom 3 — No Abstract Escalation  
Energy, force, speed, pressure, and heat are bounded by material reality, not software policy.

### Axiom 4 — Zero-Trust Cognition  
All cognitive outputs are treated as untrusted suggestions until validated by physical invariants.

---

## 5. Core Components

- **Sensorium Lite**  
  Present-only perception system. No imagination, no simulation.

- **Dizer (Distributed Invariants)**  
  Local enforcement of physical thresholds and systemic coherence.

- **SYF (Systemic Yield Framework)**  
  Measures invariant stability and detects transitions toward instability.

- **Dizer-Skin**  
  The physical boundary where force, energy, and interaction are saturated or dissipated.

---

## 6. Safety Model

Safety is achieved through **structural impossibility**, not alignment.

| Traditional AI Safety | Anathema |
|----------------------|----------|
| Align values | Enforce limits |
| Prevent bad decisions | Prevent bad actions |
| Control behavior | Saturate capability |
| Trust cognition | Trust matter |

---

## 7. Network Independence

Anathema is **offline-first** by design.

- No cloud dependency for operation
- Network access is optional, limited, and non-authoritative
- Updates cannot modify physical invariants

---

## 8. Scope

This specification defines:
- Ontology
- Safety model
- Architectural constraints

It does **not** define:
- User interfaces
- Economic systems
- Social behavior
- Consciousness claims

---

## 9. Position on Superintelligence

Anathema does not attempt to control superintelligence.

It renders superintelligence **harmless by embodiment**.

A system may think infinitely —  
but it can only act finitely.

---

## 10. Status

This document is **foundational** and **non-negotiable**.

All future implementations, simulations, or extensions of Anathema must conform to these principles.

---

**End of Foundational Specification**
