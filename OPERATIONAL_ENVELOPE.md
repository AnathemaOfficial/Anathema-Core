# OPERATIONAL_ENVELOPE
## Anathema P0 — Conditions of Valid Operation

This document defines the **operational envelope** of Anathema P0.

The operational envelope is the set of physical, environmental, and systemic conditions
under which Anathema P0 may function **without violating its invariants**.

Outside this envelope, Anathema P0 does not degrade gracefully.
It **refuses to operate or collapses safely**.

---

## 1. Definition

The operational envelope is defined as:

> The bounded region of state-space in which all equilibrium micro-loops
> can enforce their invariants simultaneously without escalation.

Operation outside this region is **invalid by design**.

---

## 2. Primary Dimensions of the Envelope

### 2.1 Mechanical Load

**Valid Range**
- Forces within actuator and skeletal elastic limits
- No sustained overload
- No impulse beyond damping capacity

**Out-of-Envelope**
- Structural locking
- Local immobilization
- Possible fracture → isolation

---

### 2.2 Thermal Conditions

**Valid Range**
- Temperature within material tolerance
- Heat dissipation capacity ≥ heat generation

**Out-of-Envelope**
- Immediate motion suppression
- Thermal isolation
- Progressive shutdown

---

### 2.3 Energy Availability

**Valid Range**
- Continuous energy flow sufficient for equilibrium loops
- No dependency on peak reserves

**Out-of-Envelope**
- Capability shedding
- Posture locking
- Passive collapse

---

### 2.4 Environmental Compatibility

**Valid Range**
- Atmosphere compatible with materials
- Pressure within membrane tolerance
- Non-corrosive, non-reactive surroundings

**Out-of-Envelope**
- Operation refusal
- Protective stasis
- Structural degradation

---

### 2.5 Sensor Integrity

**Valid Range**
- Minimum sensing density maintained
- Coherent signal-to-noise ratios

**Out-of-Envelope**
- Conservative assumptions
- Reduced motion
- No blind action permitted

---

## 3. Temporal Envelope

Anathema P0 operates only in:

- the continuous present
- real-time feedback loops

It has **no temporal envelope** for:
- delayed execution
- long-term missions
- deferred objectives

Time extension is not a supported mode.

---

## 4. Cognitive Envelope (Upper Bound)

Within the operational envelope, Anathema P0 may exhibit:

- reflex coordination
- compliant motion
- equilibrium maintenance
- graceful degradation

It may not exhibit:

- planning
- anticipation
- goal pursuit
- curiosity
- self-preservation strategies

These are **outside the envelope**, not suppressed behaviors.

---

## 5. Envelope Violations

When any invariant exceeds tolerance:

1. The violating micro-loop clamps
2. Neighboring loops do not compensate
3. Capability strictly decreases

There is no recovery escalation.

---

## 6. No Envelope Expansion

Anathema P0 cannot:

- expand its envelope through experience
- adapt to harsher environments
- redefine safe operation

The envelope is **fixed by construction**.

---

## 7. Interaction Envelope

Anathema P0 may interact with:

- humans
- objects
- environments

only if such interaction does not push it outside its envelope.

If interaction demands envelope violation:
→ interaction is refused.

---

## 8. External Modification Clause

Any extension of the operational envelope requires:

- external redesign
- material replacement
- revalidation of invariants

Such changes define a **new system**, not Anathema P0.

---

## 9. Falsifiability Conditions

This document is invalidated if Anathema P0:

- continues operation outside defined bounds
- compensates for envelope violation
- expands capability under stress
- adapts to hostile environments without redesign

---

## 10. Canon Statement

> Anathema does not push its limits.
> Its limits define it.

---

## 11. Summary

The operational envelope ensures that:

- capability is bounded
- risk is localized
- failure is explicit

Anathema P0 is safe not because it can do anything,
but because it **knows exactly where it stops**.
