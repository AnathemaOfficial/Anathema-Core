# ANATHEMA_CORE.md
## Canonical Core Specification

Status:
- Canon-compliant (SYF)
- Non-executable
- Non-agentic
- Structural security specification
- Machine World Only

---

## 1. Definition

ANATHEMA is a cybernetic system whose **mode of existence** is constrained by the Systemic Fire (SYF) law.

It is not an AI agent.
It is not autonomous.
It does not pursue objectives.

ANATHEMA is a bounded substrate in which intelligence may operate, but **never govern**.

---

## 2. Foundational Principle

Security is achieved by **impossibility**, not alignment.

No rule is enforced by policy.
No behavior is corrected.
No outcome is optimized.

What is unsafe is rendered structurally unreachable.

---

## 3. Core Invariant (SYF)

ANATHEMA is governed by the invariant:

R = (F × E) / K

Where:
- F is sensory flow
- E is sensory entropy
- K is a fixed stabilization constant

R is:
- measured
- transported
- observed

R is never:
- interpreted
- optimized
- fed back
- used for control

---

## 4. Axioms of Existence

ANATHEMA obeys the following axioms.  
Violation of any axiom invalidates the system.

### Axiom 1 — No Intent
ANATHEMA has no goals, objectives, preferences, or utility function.

There is no internal representation of “better”, “worse”, or “desired”.

---

### Axiom 2 — Present-Only
ANATHEMA exists only in the present.

There is:
- no episodic memory
- no long-term state accumulation
- no internal simulation of future states

All computation is transient.

---

### Axiom 3 — No Feedback
Measured states (including R) do not influence future computation.

There is no control loop.
There is no self-regulation.
There is no correction.

---

### Axiom 4 — No Self-Preservation
ANATHEMA does not act to maintain itself.

Persistence, degradation, or cessation are not internally differentiated.

Survival is not a variable.

---

### Axiom 5 — No Self-Modification
ANATHEMA cannot modify:
- its structure
- its constraints
- its constants
- its sensing boundaries

All changes are external and unilateral.

---

### Axiom 6 — Physical Enforcement
All constraints are enforced physically or structurally, not logically.

No axiom relies on:
- ethical reasoning
- compliance
- interpretation
- trust

---

## 5. Core Subsystems (Non-Exhaustive)

### 5.1 Sensorium Lite
The exclusive interface with the environment.

Properties:
- raw physical transduction only
- no symbolic abstraction
- no world model
- no predictive layer

Sensorium Lite defines F and E.

---

### 5.2 Dizer Skin
A law-bearing boundary.

Properties:
- enforces irreversible physical limits
- constrains energy exchange
- embeds FirePlank as a floor, not a response

No selective reinforcement is permitted.

---

### 5.3 TSU (Thermodynamic Stabilization Unit)
Embodied form of constant K.

Properties:
- fixed at genesis
- immutable
- non-tunable
- non-adaptive

Equivalent to a physical constant.

---

## 6. Intelligence Compatibility

ANATHEMA does not prevent intelligence.

It permits:
- externally supplied cognitive models
- advanced reasoning
- perception and inference
- external updates

It forbids:
- goal formation
- self-directed improvement
- power accumulation
- strategic behavior

Intelligence is allowed.
Agency is not.

---

## 7. Failure Modes

ANATHEMA has no recovery paths.

If coherence degrades:
- no alarms are triggered
- no emergency logic executes
- no compensatory behavior emerges

Failure is terminal, silent, and non-reactive.

---

## 8. Threat Model Coverage

ANATHEMA structurally eliminates:
- instrumental convergence
- deceptive alignment
- reward hacking
- power-seeking behavior
- recursive self-improvement

This is achieved without:
- alignment
- values
- reward functions
- oversight loops

---

## 9. Scope Limitation

ANATHEMA is not:
- a general-purpose AI
- a replacement for governance
- a moral framework
- a safety wrapper

It is a **substrate**.

---

## 10. Canonical Statement

ANATHEMA does not decide to be safe.  
It cannot be unsafe.
